# Project Requirement Document
## Website for Insurance Product for Expats in Thailand

## 1. Project Overview
This project aims to create a modern, user-friendly website for an insurance product designed especially for expatriates living in Thailand. The website should help potential customers understand the benefits of the product, build trust, and easily request a quote or consultation.

## 2. Purpose of the Website
The website will serve as a digital platform to:
- Explain the insurance product in simple and clear language
- Highlight benefits for expats, such as health, accident, travel, and family protection
- Encourage visitors to contact the company or request a quote
- Build credibility through testimonials, FAQs, and clear policy information

## 3. Problem Statement
Many expatriates in Thailand find insurance information difficult to understand, especially when it is written in complex legal language or not tailored to their lifestyle. They often need a product that is easy to understand, affordable, and relevant to living and working abroad.

## 4. Target Audience
Primary users:
- Foreign professionals working in Thailand
- Expats living with family members
- Digital users who prefer online research before buying

Secondary users:
- Relocating employees
- Travelers and short-term visitors
- Families looking for secure healthcare and protection options

## 5. Main Goals
- Increase awareness of the insurance product
- Generate qualified leads through online forms
- Improve trust and credibility for the brand
- Make the website easy to use on mobile and desktop devices
- Support both English and Thai language content

## 6. Website Objectives
The website should:
- Provide a clear overview of the insurance product
- Explain coverage, eligibility, and key benefits
- Offer a simple quote request or contact form
- Display FAQs and helpful information for expats
- Encourage visitors to take action, such as requesting a consultation

## 7. Core Features
### 7.1 Landing Page
- Strong headline explaining the insurance solution for expats
- Short introduction of key benefits
- Clear call-to-action buttons such as “Get a Quote” and “Contact Us”
- Visuals showing trust, healthcare, protection, and lifestyle relevance

### 7.2 Product Information Pages
- Overview of insurance plans
- Coverage details
- Eligibility and requirements
- Key exclusions and terms

### 7.3 Quote / Consultation Form
- Name
- Email
- Phone number
- Country of residence
- Preferred plan or interest area
- Optional message box

### 7.4 Trust and Support Section
- Customer testimonials
- Partner or company certifications
- FAQ section
- Contact details and WhatsApp/chat option

### 7.5 Blog or Helpful Resources
- Articles about insurance for expats in Thailand
- Tips for healthcare, travel, and financial protection
- Frequently asked questions

## 8. Content Requirements
The website content should be:
- Simple and easy to read
- Written in clear English, with optional Thai support
- Focused on benefits and peace of mind
- Free from confusing insurance jargon
- Suitable for both first-time visitors and returning users

## 9. Functional Requirements
The website must:
- Be responsive on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- Allow users to submit a quote request or contact form
- Display product information clearly and consistently
- Show contact information and support channels
- Include SEO-friendly page titles and meta descriptions
- Be secure for online forms and user data

## 10. Non-Functional Requirements
- Fast loading speed
- Easy navigation and intuitive layout
- Accessible design for users with disabilities
- Secure data handling and privacy compliance
- Clean and professional visual style

## 11. Design Direction
The website should have:
- A modern and trustworthy visual style
- Calm colors such as blue, white, and green
- Clear typography and readable layouts
- Icons for healthcare, safety, and protection
- Strong use of trust signals and customer-focused messaging

## 12. User Stories
- As an expat, I want to understand the insurance benefits quickly so I can decide if it is relevant to me.
- As a potential customer, I want to request a quote easily without filling out too many fields.
- As a visitor, I want to see customer reviews and FAQs so I can trust the company.
- As a mobile user, I want the website to work smoothly on my phone.

## 13. Success Metrics
The website will be considered successful if it achieves:
- Increased traffic from relevant online searches
- Higher number of quote requests or consultation inquiries
- Lower bounce rate from landing pages
- Stronger user engagement on key pages
- Positive feedback from users about clarity and trust

## 14. Assumptions and Risks
Assumptions:
- The company has approved product information and legal wording
- The website will be promoted through digital marketing channels

Risks:
- Insurance regulations may require careful wording
- Users may need more detailed explanation before purchasing
- Competitors may already have strong online presence

## 15. Project Summary
This website should act as a professional and trustworthy digital gateway for insurance products targeted at expatriates in Thailand. It must be clear, informative, easy to use, and designed to turn visitors into potential customers through simple and reassuring user experience.
